About Us

-A bunch of students working towards making a green future since 2010

We are not an NGO, We are a group of volunteers, who decided to work for Trees. We have a clear mission “Plant a Tree, Grow a Tree, Save Future, Save Earth”. These days we are spreading our message on social media to connect with those people who want to do something for Trees.


This initiative was started by a bunch of college students, a group of 20 members, in 2010, but now in 2021 we have more than 20k members of all age groups standing with us.


Because we know all living beings, including human being can’t live without oxygen. So in the first stage we connect with all those people, collect their suggestions and then finalize the best working ways. You can also help us by providing your valuable feedback, Please write us on xyz_plant_trees@gmail.com .


We respect your valuable feedback and if your suggestion is shortlisted, then we will list your name on our website.
